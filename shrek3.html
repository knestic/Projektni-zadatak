Shrek the Third

Synopsis

When Shrek's father-in-law, King Harold, falls ill, Shrek is looked at as the heir to the land of Far, Far Away. Not one to give up his beloved swamp, Shrek recruits his friends Donkey and Puss in Boots to install the rebellious Artie as the new king. Princess Fiona, however, rallies a group of fairy tale villains to fight for the throne and the villains and heroes face off in an epic battle.

Cast

  • Mike Myers as Shrek
  • Eddie Murphy as Donkey
  • Cameron Diaz as Princess Fiona
  • Antonio Banderas as Puss in Boots
  • Eric Idle as Merlin
  • Julie Andrews as Queen Lillian
  • John Cleese as King Harold
  • Justin Timberlake as Artie

Production

Shrek the Third was produced by DreamWorks Animation and released in 2007. It was directed by Chris Miller and written by Jeffrey Price, Peter S. Seaman, and Tom Wheeler. The film received mixed reviews and grossed over $798 million at the box office.

Awards

  • Annie Award for Best Music in an Animated Feature Production
  • Golden Globe Award for Best Animated Feature Film
