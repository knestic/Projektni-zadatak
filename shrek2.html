Shrek 2

Synopsis

Shrek and Fiona are now married and living in the swamp. When Fiona's parents invite her and Shrek to a royal ball, they are not pleased. However, they soon find out that Fiona's parents are not who they thought they were, and they must team up with old friends to defeat a new villain and save Fiona's family.

Cast

  • Mike Myers as Shrek
  • Eddie Murphy as Donkey
  • Cameron Diaz as Princess Fiona
  • Julie Andrews as Queen Lillian
  • John Cleese as King Harold

Production

Shrek 2 was produced by DreamWorks Animation and released in 2004. It was directed by Andrew Adamson, Kelly Asbury, and Conrad Vernon, and written by Adamson, Jenson, Elliott, Rossio, and Stillman. The film was a critical and commercial success, grossing over $919 million at the box office and receiving positive reviews for its animation, voice acting, and humor.

Awards

  • Academy Award for Best Animated Feature
  • Golden Globe Award for Best Animated Feature Film
  • BAFTA Award for Best Children's Film
